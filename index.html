What are Self-Help Groups?

Understanding the power of collective empowerment

Definition & Purpose

Self-Help Groups (SHGs) are informal associations of 10-15 women from similar economic backgrounds who come together to solve their common problems through self-help and mutual aid.

How SHGs Work

  • Regular savings by all members
  • Internal lending from pooled savings
  • Mutual support and guidance
  • Linkage with banks for credit
